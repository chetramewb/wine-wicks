About

Wine Wicks & Gifts...

All of our signature wine bottles, wine bottle decorations and wine bottle crafts have a unique story to tell and fit perfectly for any home decor. They are great for parties, as centerpieces for an upcoming wedding or just for display around your home. Each wine bottle that we use is recycled from a local winery and has a unique story all to its own.
